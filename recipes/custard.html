Simple Scones

Description

Delicious tender scones with juicy raisins. This sweet scone recipe is easy to make with simple baking ingredients, butter, and sour cream.

Perfect for a holiday breakfast, brunch, or afternoon treat with a cup of tea!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 4 large eggs
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch

Steps

  1. Place milk, vanilla extract, and butter in a saucepan.
  2. Cook and stir over medium heat until simmering; remove from heat before it comes to a boil.
  3. Whisk eggs, sugar, and cornstarch together in a bowl until the sugar dissolves.
  4. Set the saucepan back over low heat. Pour in the egg mixture slowly, whisking constantly, until the custard thickens enough to coat the bottom of a spoon, 5 to 10 minutes.
  5. Serve warm or transfer custard into a large bowl and cover.
  6. Allow to cool before chilling. Enjoy!
